# [Module/Programme Name] — Nurse Educator

## NurseStack
Claude Code commands for nurse educators — built by Clinical Quality Artificial Intelligence.

You are a nurse educator, practice educator, or academic. Use these commands to design, review, and improve nursing education.

## Your Commands

### 🎓 Teaching & Design
- `/plan-lesson` — Design a lesson plan mapped to NMC Standards of Proficiency
- `/review-simulation` — Audit a simulation scenario for clinical accuracy and pedagogy
- `/review-curriculum` — Map a curriculum document to NMC, NHS 10-Year Plan, and digital health
- `/review-feedback` — Structure high-quality formative and summative feedback

### 🔬 Research Support
- `/plan-dissertation` — Help students structure a dissertation
- `/plan-funding` — Structure an NIHR / HEE funding application
- `/review-research-ethics` — Check a study for NHS ethics compliance

### 🩺 Clinical Quality
- `/plan-clinical-review` — NMC-aligned product/service review
- `/review-clinical-safety` — DCB0129/DCB0160 clinical safety audit
- `/review-fhir` — FHIR IG and clinical data standards compliance

## Context
All teaching materials must align to NMC Standards of Proficiency for Registered Nurses (2018).
All simulations must include a structured debrief plan.
All student tools must include the clinical disclaimer.
